Usage

  1. Open any page, then click the Scroller bookmarklet.
  2. Hover the circular control in the bottom-left corner to expand it.
  3. Use play or pause, adjust the speed slider, or flip the arrow to reverse direction instantly.
  4. Close the widget to remove the entire UI and stop the animation loop.
