Drag this button to your bookmarks bar:

❌ Thread Scraper

Click and drag the button above to your bookmarks bar

Usage

  1. Open the tweet thread page on X (Twitter)
  2. Click the ❌ Thread Scraper bookmarklet. A floating Copy … tweets button appears
  3. Let it auto-scroll for a bit, or scroll yourself to load more replies
  4. Click the button to copy all captured tweets to your clipboard
